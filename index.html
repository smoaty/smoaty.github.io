ABOUT ME

Finally finding what makes me tick

After five long years in the mortgage industry, I knew I was ready for a change. Nearly two years later, I finally made one. It was one of the best decisions I've ever made in my life. I left behind my comfortable, yet not fulfilling, Loan Officer career and headed off to a full time coding bootcamp with a two month old son at home and no guarantee of a job at the end. Am I crazy? A little. But I have a wonderfully supportive wife who helped push me forward because she knew more than anything else I wanted to find a career that would simultaneously fulfill my desire to be creative and inventive.

I knew immediately that I made the right decision. Web Design and Software Development scratch that deep creative itch. The desire to create something out of nothing. Something that not only looks good, but functions in a way that makes sense. Things that are designed in such a way, that you never feel like you had to learn to use them. In learning to write code, I've started looking at everything around me differently. It has been an overwhelmingly positive experience.
